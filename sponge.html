Statement of Interest

I have a strong interest in visual, and fractal is one of the cool geometry patterns that draws my attention. Combining my knowledge and skills in combinatorics, programming, 3D modeling and 3D printing, i made this website to demonstrate a step-by-step process of how I modeled level 2 & level 3 Menger's Sponge and 3D printed them as a cool-looking decorative object.
